How Blydeverify Works

The process a product goes through to ensure authenticity

  • Product Labelling

    Each product is labelled with Blyde Verify tag

  • Anti-corruptible

    We ensure that each product is validated and recorded prevent even companies from counterfeiting their own goods

  • Verified Supply

    Each product is verified along the supply line. Supply chain becomes transparent to the extent we want it to be

  • Retail Verification

    Retail locations can use mobile devices for verification. They can be assured that the goods they receive are genuine

  • Consumer Verified

    When the consumer purchases a product he/she is able to verify that the product is genuine and activate it

  • Blyde Verified

    Each product has a recorded history permanently recorded in the Blydechain. We can provide verified history for each product
